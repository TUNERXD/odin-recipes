Lemon Pasta Recipes

Description

A simple lemon pasta recipe made with spaghetti, garlic, fresh lemon, and Parmesan cheese, making it creamy without cream, great for a quick weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Pasta
  • Herbs and seasonings
  • Lemon
  • Cheese

How to make Cherry Tomato Pasta

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet and add the garlic, parsley, and crushed red pepper. Cook, then add the lemon zest, lemon juice, and salt.
  2. Add the al dente spaghetti and reserved pasta water. Cook until the pasta heats through.
  3. Turn off the heat. Then, stir in the Parmesan cheese until it clings to the pasta.
  4. Serve with extra parsley and Parmesan cheese, if desired.
